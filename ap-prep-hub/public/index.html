Apex Scholar — AI-Powered AP Exam Prep Platform

Free, all-in-one study tool for Advanced Placement exams. Apex Scholar combines AI tutors for every AP subject, AI-generated practice tests with rubric-aware scoring and a predicted 1–5 score, a Smart Scheduler that integrates with Schoology to plan study blocks around your real assignments and free time, flashcards with spaced repetition and shareable public decks, a photo and text problem solver that returns step-by-step solutions in LaTeX, and unit-by-unit diagnostics that map your strengths and weaknesses across each AP unit.

Features

  • AI Tutors — chat one-on-one with subject-trained AI tutors covering AP Calculus AB & BC, AP Biology, AP Chemistry, AP Physics 1 & C, AP Statistics, AP US History, AP World History, AP European History, AP Government, AP English Language, AP English Literature, AP Psychology, AP Computer Science A & Principles, AP Environmental Science, AP Macro & Microeconomics, AP Human Geography, and more.
  • Smart Scheduler — auto-generates focused study blocks based on your AP exam dates, Schoology assignments, blackout schedule, and study preferences (session length, peak hours, weekend availability).
  • Practice Tests — unlimited AI-generated AP-style multiple-choice and free-response questions, graded against the official College Board rubric with a predicted 1–5 score.
  • Flashcards — generate decks from your notes, study with spaced repetition, share public decks with classmates.
  • Solver — snap a photo or paste text; the solver returns step-by-step LaTeX-rendered solutions for math, physics, chemistry, and more.
  • Diagnostics — short assessments that map your mastery of each AP unit, then recommend exactly which units to study next.

Why Apex Scholar

Built specifically for AP students by an AP student. Free with no trial period. Combines what would otherwise require Khan Academy, Albert.io, Quizlet, Anki, Photomath, and a study planner into one app — all powered by AI tutors that understand the College Board frameworks for every AP subject.
